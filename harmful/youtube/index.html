Youtube has a distraction

Many people come to youtube to watch videos from an exact channel, or even one video. However, when one is watching a video, he sees "recommendations" on the right and clicks on them, and thus starts roaming from one video to another, forgetting the purpose of going to Youtube in the first place.
Besides people afromentioned, there are more out there who open Youtube just after booting a computer without even a rigorous reason to do so. They may perceive "recommendations" as a good thing, because otherwise they would have nothing to do.

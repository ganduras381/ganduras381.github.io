Freedom from harmful technology

Modern technology, contrarily to popular belief, makes us only weaker and dumber. I use the word 'us' as individual human beings, or you. A good example of this are elevators in multi-storey buildings. Have I become too weak to use the stairs with my foot? What if the elevator is broken and I live on 26-th floor?
"Is my memory so weak that I can't remember ten items to buy in the store?" If the answer is yes, you need a shopping list.
Technology breaks very often, sometimes because the creator wants them to. When it happens, people tend and encouraged (by companies) to call somebody to do the repair for them instead of repairing by themselves, thus they are dependent on the guy who repairs (often called an "expert"). You also need to pay the "expert" (if you don't, his service is included in the price of the item), forcing you to work on the "job" more, if you are not working enough already. Disability to repair one's own piece of technology makes him dependent even more.

The more advanced the man, the less technology he needs
